Selling Options To Consider

Option A – Traditional Sale

  • Multiple showings and days/weeks until sold
  • Home inspections
  • Repair requests from buyer to be done at seller expense
  • Close date picked by buyer (±60+ day close)
  • More hassle but possibly more cash at closing

Option B – Sell To A Cash Investor

  • Quick close, you pick the closing date
  • No repairs, “as-is” condition
  • No inspection (municipal inspections if applicable i.e. inflow & infiltration)
  • Less hassle and possibly less cash at closing
